ACKOSA 49th Anniversary & 13th Speech and Prize Giving Day

Theme: Unearthing and Nurturing Real Talents, through 21st Century Skills: The role of stakeholders

This year's celebration marks 49 years of academic excellence and transformation. It will also feature the 13th Speech and Prize Giving Day, honoring hardworking students, dedicated teachers, and supportive stakeholders who continue to shape the legacy of ACKOSA.

×

Program of Activities for the 49th Anniversary

  • 12th Oct: Church Service at Christ Cathedral Anglican Church
  • 13th Oct: Akwaaba Convoy & Candle Light Procession
  • 14th Oct: Health Screening & Interaction with Teachers and Students
  • 15th Oct: ACKOSA Back to School & Fun Games
  • 16th Oct: Float from Old Site to New Site
  • 17th Oct: Welcome Fresh Students + Anniversary Eve Get Together
  • 18th Oct: 13th Speech & Prize Giving Day + 49th Anniversary + Dinner Dance
  • 19th Oct: Thanksgiving Service & "Machepa" Party
