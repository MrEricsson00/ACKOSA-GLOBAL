About ACKOSA

The Academy Of Christ The King Old Students Association (ACKOSA) was established to unite alumni from all graduating classes, foster lifelong connections, and support the continued development of our beloved alma mater.

Our mission is to create a vibrant network that promotes professional development, mentorship opportunities, and philanthropic initiatives that benefit current students and the school community.
